About Us

Richmond Brand Advertising Limited is a Zambian company with it's parent company in Mauritius. One of the leading entities in providing synergies in advertising.

Services

What We Do.

We are one of the leading entities in the country providing synergies in advertising by partnering with local authorities and especially with the local people in the communities we operate. Our international base line endows us with vast experience that enables us to perfect our services to our clients and form partnerships to foster business and development in Africa.

BillBoard Advertising

Billboards are ideal for new product launches and as a long-term brand building strategy - they deliver high reach, and create impact for your ad campaigns. Our diverse range of available sites allows you to target specific markets, thereby minimising advertising wastage.

Landmark Advertising

Landmark Outdoor sites provide maximum impact for the advertised brand. This creates market dominance through the billboard’s unusual size and the location of the site. Building wraps, wall sites, bridge advertising, rooftop billboards and custom outdoor advertising installations form part of this advertising category.

Street Advertising

Street Advertising provides the ideal opportunity to drive your brand into the minds of all commuters, pedestrians and motorists. An added bonus is the positive contribution this medium provides to the community. This easily accessible and affordable media includes: Bus Shelters, Suburb Signs, Street Finders and Street Pole Advertising.

Shopping Mall Media

Shopping Mall media talks directly to the consumer with limited media wastage. It creates a dominating and influential presence targeting commuters and shopping decision makers alike. It is an ideal opportunity to attract consumers in a targeted retail environment. Because of the strategic locations of the adverts, mall media showcases your brand where buying decisions are being made.

Airport Media

Airport advertising provides an ideal platform to target influential decision makers with little media wastage. This premium environment enhances a brand's stature and assists in building an internationally recognised brand. Richmond Outdoor Adcertising has a vast array of innovative airport advertising options available within the international and domestic airport terminal buildings, as well as outside the airport, both airside and landside.

Remarkable Media

Remarkable Media establish your brand as the market leader. Situated in premium locations these structures are designed to capture and captivate the viewer. This spectacular category includes the following products: the Advertising Gantry, the Cantilever, the 12 x 10 portrait billboard (120 square metres), the 5 x 20 landscape billboard (100 square metres) and the the enormous 15 x 12.5 portrait billboard - that equates to 188 square metres of advertising space!
